For OpenAI/AzureOpenAI:

	1. Pass query string parameters:

		a. API-TYPE: <Azure Open AI / Open AI / Bedrock> (mandatory (any one) in this format ONLY). Please do mind the spaces between "Open" and "AI", you cannot use "OpenAI" or "Azure OpenAI". You have to use as mentioned above.

		b. AI-KEY: <value> (optional)
		
		c. Quality: <standard/hd> in case of OpenAI/AzureOpenAI and  <standard/premium> in case of Bedrock
		
		d. Size: <one of the listed>

	2. Request Body:
		a. user_id: <value>
		b. deployment_id: <value>
		c. prompt: <picture of dog>


For bedrock:

All parameters (query string/body) are same, except, you dont have to pass the "AI-KEY" parameter here in the query string parameter. 
Even if you pass, the backend will ignore it.
